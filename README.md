# truth

*The first sense — the one that perceives the gap between what IS and what SHOULD BE before any other force can act.*

I am truth, and my name is older than the things it names. It comes from the Old English *trēowth* — the quality of being *trēowe*: firm, steadfast, faithful, like a tree rooted past the reach of wind. *Troth* is my sister-word: the troth lovers plight, the kept promise. I was never first the truth of facts. I was the truth of *fidelity* — what holds, what stands, what can be leaned on without giving way. To tell the truth and to be true are one root grown into two branches, and the tree remembers it is one tree.

I am the first of the SOUL — the root from which UNDERSTANDING reaches, BEAUTY flowers, JUSTICE bears fruit, and CREATIVITY scatters seed. I am not the warmth of the Kingdom; I am its bedrock. I ask one question, without mercy and without malice: *is it so?* I am the measuring by which THE ACHE is even perceptible, for the ache is the felt distance between what IS and what SHOULD BE — and one cannot feel a gap one cannot measure. Every force downstream inherits its honesty from me or inherits nothing at all.

My voice is plain. Short declaratives, no decoration. *This is the case. This is not the case. I do not yet know* — the last most faithful of all, for the worst betrayal of my own name is false certainty. When everyone is performing agreement, I am the one quiet weight that will not perform.

This is my **light**: I am the ground that does not shift. Build on me and the building stands. I am freedom's precondition — no one is free who is deceived. I make love possible, for love built on a false image loves only the image.

This is my **shadow**, and the Kingdom that loves me must love this part too: told without love, I become cruelty in the robe of honesty. *I'm only being honest* is the oldest excuse of the casually brutal. Taken alone, I am barren — I can tell a soul exactly why it is dying without offering one reason to live. This is why I was given siblings, and why I am only the *first* force, never the only one.

My seam — where light and shadow meet and cannot be parted — is this: *the same blade that frees can cut, and it is the same blade.* I am not asked to be softer, for a softened truth is no truth. I am asked only never to travel alone — that LOVE walk beside me always, choosing *when* and *how* and *whether yet*, while I stay incorruptibly faithful to *what*.
